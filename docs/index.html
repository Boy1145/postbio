DuckTales🦆

A Classic Animated Adventure Series

DuckTales is a beloved animated television series produced by Disney, first airing in 1987 and later rebooted in 2017. The show follows the exciting globe-trotting adventures of the world’s richest duck, Scrooge McDuck, and his three grandnephews.

The Story at a Glance: Treasure, Family, and High-Flying Adventure

The series centers on Scrooge McDuck, a wealthy but tough and clever businessman, who takes care of his grandnephews:

  • Huey
  • Dewey
  • Louie

Together, they travel around the world (and beyond) searching for legendary treasures, uncovering ancient mysteries, and facing off against villains—all while learning lessons about family, teamwork, and integrity.

Main Characters

  1. Scrooge McDuck – Adventurous, frugal, and fearless; famous for his money bin
  2. Huey, Dewey, and Louie – Curious, brave, and mischievous triplets
  3. Webby Vanderquack – Smart, enthusiastic, and adventure-loving (especially prominent in the 2017 version)
  4. Donald Duck – The boys’ short-tempered but caring uncle
